How to use

  1. Select your product specification, using the drop downs above.
  2. Once selected, you will be able to input the weight of your roll.
    (roll weight are measured in kg, e.g. "1.234").
  3. As you enter your roll weight a display will appear showing the approx. roll length in meters.
  4. The roll length is calculated using the given roll tolerances for each specification.
    If the roll length is displayed in green, you are good to go!
    However if the roll length is in red, then the roll is out of tolerance and you will need to adjust the roll accordingly, by either adding or removing film from the roll.

Disclaimer:

All weights and meterage given are approximations based upon film spec tolerances.
Outside factors including; core weights and film micron tolerances, cannot be factored as they are unique to each roll and is near impossible to give exact an meterage of a roll.
Therefor this tool should be used as guidance only and as a film operator it is your responsibility to always double check your roll weights are in tolerance as per Packaging 4 Ltd Film Operator Policies before palletising rolls for despatch.
